Korean Kimchi Stew (Kimchi-jjigae)

Description

In Korean cuisine, kimchi is not only a condiment or pickle; it is also as an ingredient in many cooked dishes. Kimchi-jjigae, is a satisfying example. Kimchi-jjigae or kimchi stew is a jjigae, or stew-like Korean dish, made with kimchi and other ingredients, such as pork or, scallions, onions, and diced tofu. It is one of the most common stews in Korean cuisine.
